## FIRE ALARMS & SUPPRESSION SYSTEMS

FSS offers the latest technology in as far as fire alarm & suppression systems are concerned. Our systems
have devices that detect and warn people through visual and audio appliances. Our different systems are
designed to detect smoke, heat, carbon dioxide or other emergencies present and have the capacity to
extinguish the hazard in the absence of humans. These alarm system maybe activated automatically
from smoke detectors & heat detectors or may also be activated manually via manual call points.
Our strobes ( Speakers ) which sound an alarm , can be set to certain frequences & different fones depe-
nding on the noise volumes of the areas & customer requirements & all this helps to ensure total evacuation
incase of an emergency.
Our suppression system conform to latest environmental standards as we offer a choice of Stat-x, FM200, CO-.
systems.Our are easily maintained & come with the latest panels so you can rest assure your high value equip-
ment is well protected.

## AIR CONDITIONING

FSS has created a name in the climate control industry by being a supplier of top-quality temperature control
devices. We are suppliers of top-quality brands i.e LG, Samsung, Rowa to mention but a few. It is our belief that
our customers deserve only the best quality products.
With a dedicated staff, we believe in understanding our customer needs and meeting their requirements at the
best prices thus ensuring we save our customer'’s resources. FSS serves both domestic and corporate clients hence
our products meet the highest expected standards.
We supply and service air conditioning units for use in homes, offices, schools, hotels, canteens and kitchens. FSS
offers both wall and ceiling options in accordance to customer requirements.
Our service and maintenance department, assures you quick turn around time for all spares, whilst
ensuring that there is no compromise on quality. Our service department has back up spares for
more than 100 line items for both commmercial and domestic systems.

## CCTV/ACCESS CONTROL

We are a one stop shop in the Security industry and our CCTV department has integrated access
control to ensure smarter home and office security. Our CCTV and Access control department not
only ensures our customers a piece of mind but also ensure that they have total control of their
businesses or homes even during their absence.
We supply intergrated CCTV, Access control as well as alarm security in accordance to customer
requirements so ypu can keep your loved ones and property safe. Our systems are unique because
they not only provide footage but they provide an alarm in the event of a breach hence enabling

Our systems can be paired so as to help generate video data that is smart, and are able to offer facial
recognition. Our smart systems are aimed at matching latest international standards hence ensuring
that we stay ahead of latest intruder tack ticks.

## FIRE SPRINKLERS

FSS has a dedicated engineering department that has more than 20 years working experience in the
fire sprinkler system industry. Fire sprinkler systems are a form of active fire protection method, these
systems can either be wet or dry, depending on the building usage. The sprinkler systems supply enough
pressurized water, through a connected piping system thus ensuring that total protection for all the floor
area.
Fire sprinkler systems are a cost effective and safe method for both domestic and commercial properties.
FSS believes in providing relevance and value to customers thus we have a department that designs, installs,
services and upgrades fire sprinkler systems, in line with modern building by-laws so that our customer's
investment is kept safe even after hours.
